Why the approximation improves with larger N: with 4 sectors, each piece is a big wedge and the rearranged shape is chunky and wavy. With 16 sectors, the shape starts to look like a parallelogram. With 64 sectors, the shape is almost perfectly smooth — the top and bottom edges approach straight lines of length πr, and the sides approach vertical lines of length r. In the limit (infinitely many sectors), the shape IS a parallelogram. That's the proof.
