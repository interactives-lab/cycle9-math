Description

A cube has 9 planes of symmetry: 3 axis-aligned (through opposite faces) plus 6 diagonal (through opposite edges). The cube's equal side lengths are what give it those extra 6 diagonal planes.

Rotation

Order 4 about a face-to-face axis. Rotation angle = 360° ÷ 4 = 90°. The cube looks identical at 0°, 90°, 180°, and 270°.
