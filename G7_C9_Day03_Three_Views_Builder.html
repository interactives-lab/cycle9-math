How to read the grid: each cell of the top-down grid is one column of cubes. The number tells you how many cubes are stacked in that column. Click to add a cube to the column; right-click (or long-press on touch) to remove one.

The Three Views

As you build on the left, the top, front, and side views render here automatically. All three views use the same scale: 1 grid square = 1 cube face.

3D View (isometric projection)

What you're seeing: the TOP view (teal) shows the footprint — what you'd see looking straight down. The FRONT view (purple) shows the silhouette from in front. The SIDE view (gold) shows the silhouette from the left. The 3D isometric view shows all three dimensions in a single drawing — that's what we'll learn to draw on Day 4.
