The Four-Step Workflow

Watch each step execute. The result of each step feeds into the next.

Step 1 — Compute Volume
V = l × w × h = 100 × 100 × 100
1,000,000 cm³
Step 2 — Convert to Capacity
1 cm³ = 1 mL → same number
1,000,000 mL = 1,000 L
Step 3 — Divide by Bottle Capacity
1,000,000 mL ÷ 500 mL/bottle
2,000 bottles
Step 4 — Sanity Check
Is this reasonable for the container size?
