Day 5 callback: if k = 2, this is the dilation rule from Day 5. Doubling a shape gives 4× the area (k²) and 8× the volume (k³). Today, k = 100 gives the m → cm conversion: 10,000× the area and 1,000,000× the volume. Same rule. Different k.
The universal rule: lengths scale by k, areas by k², volumes by k³. The exponent in the unit (², ³) tells you how many times to multiply k by itself. This is Day 5's dilation rule AND today's metric conversion rule — same math, different application.
