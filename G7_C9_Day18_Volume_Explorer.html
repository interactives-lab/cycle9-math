Active Formula
V = l × w × h
The generalization: Rectangular prism, triangular prism, cylinder. Three different shapes. Three different base-area formulas (l × w, (1/2)bh, πr²). But the outer pattern V = base × height never changes. Toggle between modes and watch the Volume card — it's computed the same way every time. One formula, infinite applications. This is how mathematicians think: find the most general statement that captures all the specific cases.
