Grade 7 — Cycle 9 Interactive Tools

Geometry, Transformations, Circles & Measurement

18 interactive visualization tools, one for each instructional day. Click any card to open the tool in your browser. All tools work on phones, tablets, and computers. No login, no account, no download.

Use these in class, at home, or for review. Each tool matches the day's lesson exactly.

Block 1 — Classifying 3D Shapes (Days 1–2)
Day 1
3D Shape Explorer
Rotate and inspect cubes, prisms, pyramids, cylinders, cones, and spheres. See faces, vertices, and edges.
Day 2
Symmetry Explorer
Find planes of symmetry in 3D shapes. Rotate and slice to see which cuts produce matching halves.
Block 2 — Views and Projections (Days 3–4)
Day 3
Three-Views Builder
Build cube arrangements and see the top, front, and side views update live. Blueprint thinking.
Day 4
Isometric Builder
Construct 3D shapes on an isometric grid. See the orthographic views alongside.
Block 3 — Dilations and Similarity (Days 5–6)
Day 5
Dilation Explorer
Scale shapes by factor k. Watch how lengths scale by k but areas scale by k². The beautiful k² rule.
Day 6
Similarity Checker
Enter two shapes' dimensions. Check whether they're similar and find the scale factor.
Block 4 — Translations, Reflections, Rotations (Days 7–8)
Day 7
Transformation Playground
Drag shapes on a coordinate grid. Apply translations and reflections. See the coordinate rules.
Day 8
Rotation Explorer
Rotate shapes 90°, 180°, and 270° around any point. Watch the rotation arcs.
Block 5 — Volume and Capacity (Days 9–10)
Day 9
Volume Builder
Adjust l, w, h sliders. Watch a 3D rectangular prism fill with water. Volume = l × w × h.
Day 10
Capacity Calculator
Convert between cm³, mL, L, and m³. The 2000-bottle tank demonstration.
Block 6 — Metric Unit Conversions (Days 11–12)
Day 11
Conversion Visualizer
Slider for k. See length, area, and volume scale by k, k², and k³. The three scaling rules.
Day 12
Conversion Chain
Multi-step conversion workflow solver. Enter a value and unit, get a chain of conversions.
Block 7 — Circles (Days 13–14)
Day 13
π Discovery Lab
Drag a circle to change its size. Watch C/d stay exactly 3.14. The mystery of π revealed.
Day 14
Compass Simulator
Three-mode compass — watch animated arc sweeps construct circles with given radius, diameter, or circumference.
Block 8 — Area of a Circle (Days 15–16)
Day 15
Parallelogram Proof
Slice a circle into N sectors and rearrange. Watch the shape approach a parallelogram. See A = πr² proved visually.
Day 16
Pizza Comparator
Compare two pizzas by cost per cm². Or compare sprinkler configurations by total area. Surprising results!
Block 9 — Cylinders (Days 17–18)
Day 17
Cylinder Unroller
Drag the unroll slider to watch a cylinder flatten into 2 circles + 1 rectangle. Surface area formulas live.
Day 18
Volume Explorer
Toggle between rectangle, triangle, and circle bases. Watch V = (base area) × height work for all three.
